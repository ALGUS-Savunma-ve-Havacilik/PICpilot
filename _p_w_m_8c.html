unsigned char getPWMInputStatus ( void  )

Returns 8-bit bit mask indicating the status of each channel.

A 0 means that the channel is functioning (connected or disabled), whilst a 1 means a channel is disconnected (only if its enabled). Therefore if the status is equal to 0, you can assume all the channels are working fine. If its greater than 0, some of the channels have disconnected. Most importantly, if the status equals 0xFF, all the channels have disconnected, which indicates that the UHF connection was lost.
